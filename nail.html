Nail Art

Nail art is a creative way to paint, decorate, enhance, and embellish the nails. It is a type of artwork that can be done on fingernails and toenails, usually after a manicure or a pedicure. A manicure and a pedicure are beauty treatments that trim, shape, and polish the nail. Often these procedures remove the cuticles and soften the skin around the nails, also can be beneficial to physical and mental health. Nail art done by itself is believed to relieve stress.These treatments are commonly performed in beauty salons, but can be done at home with some practice. Nail art is considered a fashion activity and commonly viewed as self-expression. There are no limitations to it; nail art could be anything from simple French manicure to 3-D stickers and more.
